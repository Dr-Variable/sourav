About me

Hello — I'm Sourav Dutta — a PhD student exploring the quantum realm whenever I get time off from my highly optimized procrastination routine (which, honestly, is the most stable thing in my life). When I’m not staring into the void (a.k.a. my code output pretending to be helpful), I do research in quantum information, error correction, and anything else that lets me feel productive for at least 30 seconds.
My research usually happens in short bursts of clarity, surrounded by long philosophical debates with myself about life choices, the universe, and why doing a PhD felt like a good idea at the time.
If you enjoy quantum science served with a side of unplanned existential crisis, welcome aboard..
