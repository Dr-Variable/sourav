Research Interests

My primary research interests lie in quantum information and quantum computing. During my Ph.D., I focus on developing noise-adaptive quantum error-correcting codes and related techniques. I am also interested in surface codes, the X-cube model, non-stabilizer codes, and fault tolerance. Beyond QEC, I enjoy exploring quantum key distribution, quantum chaos, and quantum algorithms.
